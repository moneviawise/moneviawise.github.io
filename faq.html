Frequently asked questions

A few quick answers about downloads, printing, and how you can use your Monevia Wise planners.

How do I download my files?

If you purchased through Etsy, your files will be available in your Etsy account under Purchases & reviews. You’ll also get an email from Etsy with download links.

Can I reuse the planner every month?

Yes. You can print pages as many times as you like for your personal use. Commercial redistribution or sharing the files is not allowed.

What size are the printables?

Most planners include both US Letter (8.5"x11") and A4 versions. Check each product description in the shop for exact details.

Can I use these on my iPad or tablet?

Yes. You can import the PDF into note-taking apps like GoodNotes, Notability, or similar apps and write on them with a stylus.
